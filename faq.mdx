## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to connect my wallet like MetaMask?">
    No! CryptoTally is **read-only**. You just paste your wallet address - no wallet connection, no signing, no risk to your funds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes! We only read public blockchain data. We never ask for private keys, seed phrases, or wallet signatures. Your funds are completely safe.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    1. Sign up at [app.cryptotally.xyz](https://app.cryptotally.xyz)
    2. Add your wallet address(es)
    3. We automatically sync all transactions
    4. Start tagging and organizing!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes! Join our [waitlist](https://app.cryptotally.xyz/waitlist) to get early access and try all features.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Wallets & Blockchains

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which blockchains are supported?">
    Currently supported:
    - Ethereum (ETH)
    - Polygon (MATIC)
    - Arbitrum
    - BNB Chain (BSC)

    More chains (Optimism, Base, Solana) coming soon!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I track multiple wallets?">
    Yes! You can add unlimited wallets across different blockchains. Perfect for managing:
    - Personal + business wallets
    - Multiple treasury wallets
    - Hot wallet + cold storage
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I track someone else's wallet?">
    Yes! Since it's read-only and uses public blockchain data, you can track any public wallet address. This is useful for:
    - Monitoring DAO treasuries
    - Tracking grants or investments
    - Following public figures
    - Auditing purposes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you support multi-sig wallets?">
    Yes! Multi-sig wallets work the same way - just paste the multi-sig address and we'll track all transactions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Transactions & Pricing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What types of transactions are tracked?">
    We track:
    - Native token transfers (ETH, MATIC, BNB)
    - ERC20 token transfers
    - NFT transfers
    - DEX swaps (Uniswap, SushiSwap, etc.)
    - Smart contract interactions
    - Internal transactions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate are the fiat values?">
    We use **CoinGecko API** to fetch historical prices at the exact timestamp of each transaction. This ensures accurate tax reporting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit transaction details?">
    You can add:
    - Custom tags
    - Notes/narration
    - Attachments (invoices, receipts)

    But the blockchain data (amounts, addresses, timestamps) cannot be edited - it's immutable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often do transactions sync?">
    - New transactions: Real-time sync
    - Historical data: Fetched immediately when you add a wallet
    - Background updates: Every 15 minutes
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tags & Organization

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are tags and why should I use them?">
    Tags help categorize transactions for:
    - **Tax filing** - separate income vs expenses
    - **Accounting** - track by category (payroll, vendors, etc.)
    - **Reporting** - filter and export by tag
    - **Grant audits** - show how funds were used
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create custom tags?">
    Yes! Create unlimited custom tags. Common examples:
    - Customer Payment
    - Vendor Expense
    - Salary
    - Grant
    - Gas Fees
    - Investment
    - Staking Rewards
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I bulk tag transactions?">
    Yes! Select multiple transactions and tag them all at once. Great for:
    - Tagging all transactions from the same wallet
    - Categorizing a month's worth of expenses
    - Processing grant payments
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What data do you collect?">
    We only collect:
    - Public blockchain transaction data
    - Tags and notes you add
    - Your email for account access

    We do NOT collect:
    - Private keys
    - Seed phrases
    - Wallet signatures
    - Personal financial data beyond what you choose to tag
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete my data?">
    Yes! You can:
    - Remove individual wallets
    - Delete your entire account
    - Export all your data before deletion

    Contact [feedback](https://app.cryptotally.xyz/feedback) for account deletion requests.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you share my data?">
    No! We never sell or share your data with third parties. Your transaction tags and notes are private to your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if a transaction is missing?">
    If you notice a missing transaction:
    1. Check if the wallet address is correct
    2. Verify the blockchain is supported
    3. Wait a few minutes for sync
    4. Report it via [feedback](https://app.cryptotally.xyz/feedback)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is my balance different from my wallet?">
    Common reasons:
    - Pending transactions not yet confirmed
    - NFT valuations (we show token balances, not NFT floor prices)
    - Cross-chain transactions still syncing
    - Unsupported tokens on that chain
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import historical data from other tools?">
    Coming soon! We're building import functionality for:
    - Koinly exports
    - CoinTracker exports
    - Generic CSV format
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you support test networks?">
    Not currently. We focus on mainnet transactions for production use. Contact us if you need testnet support for development.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
