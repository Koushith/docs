## Setup your account

Get your crypto transactions organized in just a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up with your email or Google account at [app.cryptotally.xyz](https://app.cryptotally.xyz)

    ```bash
    # Or join the waitlist first
    https://app.cryptotally.xyz/waitlist
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Wallet">
    Navigate to the **Wallets** page and click "Add Wallet"

    <Tip>
      You only need the wallet address - no private keys or signatures required!
    </Tip>

    - Paste your wallet address (0x...)
    - Select the blockchain (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
    - Add a label (e.g., "Treasury", "Personal")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sync Transactions">
    CryptoTally automatically fetches all transactions for your wallet

    <Info>
      Initial sync may take a few moments depending on transaction history
    </Info>

    Features:
    - Native token transfers (ETH, MATIC, BNB)
    - ERC20 token transfers
    - Fiat values at time of transaction
    - DEX swaps and complex transactions
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tag Your Transactions">
    Click on any transaction to add tags and notes

    Common tags:
    - Customer Payment
    - Vendor Expense
    - Salary
    - Grant
    - Gas Fees
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export for Taxes">
    Generate reports filtered by date range, wallet, or tag

    ```bash
    Dashboard → Reports → Export CSV
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Freelancer receiving crypto payments">
    1. Add your Ethereum wallet where clients pay you
    2. Tag each payment with client name or project
    3. Upload invoices as attachments
    4. Export filtered by date range for quarterly taxes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Startup managing treasury">
    1. Add your DAO treasury wallet
    2. Tag expenses (payroll, vendors, tools)
    3. Track income (investments, grants, revenue)
    4. Generate monthly reports for accounting
  </Accordion>
